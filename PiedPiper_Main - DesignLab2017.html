Problem Statement[edit]

  • Highway accidents are very common in India. Such accidents mostly involve heavy vehicles like Buses, trucks etc.
  • One of the major reason for such accidents is drivers falling asleep while driving and hence, losing control over the vehicle.

Problem Analysis Video[edit]

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=viwGTJKBneU

Solution Statement[edit]

  • A system capable of detecting whether the driver is falling asleep is a good step towards solving this problem.
  • The system will generate a sound which will alert the driver and other passengers and hence, an accident can be avoided.
